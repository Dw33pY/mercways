Welcome to Mercways Excelling Ltd

Mercways Excelling Limited is an independently owned recruitment consultancy firm, which offers unrivalled recruitment and staffing solutions for both work candidates and employers. We are at the helm of human resource sector focusing on all elements of recruitment.

About Us

Mercways Excelling Limited is one of the top growing recruitment firms with its focus on premium placement services.

We are legally registered with the ministry of labor and we engage in job placements of highly qualified trained, competent skilled and unskilled staffs globally. We commit ourselves to satisfy our clients and staffs.

Our Mission

To provide excellent, prompt, and efficient services that consistently meet needs using a combination of deep industry knowledge, integrity and experiences. We maintain the highest standards in clients and applicants care.

The Team

Our company is led by a team of recruitment experts with specialist knowledge of a whole range of markets and industries. Our consultants bring together a rare blend of years of recruitment and industry expertise which is reflected in the range of recruitment options we offer our clients and the sheer speed of our recruitment and selection process.

Providing excellent customer service and sustained support to our clients and candidates underpins our business and is an area we are always developing. Our candidates and clients benefit intensely from our extensive network of industry contacts and the specialist sector knowledge of our consultants.

What We Do

We aim to provide a tailored and professional recruitment services that helps best meet the needs of our clients. To provide strong, fast dynamic and responsive experience to our clients by searching the best candidates.

Mercways Excelling Limited aims to focus on the enhancement of client organizations by providing quality services on various aspects of their human resource and letting the organization focus on its core values. Our solutions aim in reducing employers recruitment process by searching through CVs, responding to candidates and arranging interviews. We do this by providing on-site and off-site recruiters who are dedicated in giving our clients value for their money.

Our Guiding Values
